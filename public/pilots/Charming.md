Vladimir Prendes, the sixth of his name, hails from the Prendes dynasty, a lineage of high-ranking nobles from a world within the Karrakin Trade Baronies, which is a tributary of the Union. Despite living a life of luxury, he was often bored and would frequently throw items at and boss around the staff needlessly. During his adolescence, he snuck out of his family’s estate just as a renowned lancer was passing through the city where his family held their station. Witnessing the adoration and fame the lancer garnered, young Vladimir was inspired to pursue the path of becoming a lancer himself. However, this decision led his father, Vladimir Prendes the Fifth, to disown him. Even so, Vladimir still believes he holds influence over lesser people.

Before his disownment, he managed to use his family name to gain entry into an officers' academy. Initially disappointed at not being put on the path to becoming a lancer, he was soon discovered to be a natural at piloting mechs, which led to his selection as a lancer. It was during this time, unbeknownst to him, that he was disowned. The callsign "Charming" was assigned to him by an instructor who found himself saying the word sarcastically almost every time Vladimir spoke, and the name stuck. His most notable attributes are his reaction speed and his innate understanding of ballistic calculations.

When dictating the design of his mech, he requested "the biggest possible artillery feasible, as is becoming of one of my standing."
