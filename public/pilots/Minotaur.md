## Dossier Entry: Leonard Galizia AKA "Minotaur"

Name: Leonard Galizia
Callsign: Minotaur
Affiliation: Former Spec Ops, Current Independent Lancer
Status: Active
Background: Classified (Amnesia)
Known Skills: Advanced Close Quarters Combat, Reconnaissance, Tactical Evasion, Hand-to-Hand Combat

# Physical Appearance:

Minotaur stands at 6'3", with a muscular, battle-hardened physique that speaks to years of rigorous military training. His face is weathered, with deep-set eyes that seem to hold a thousand-yard stare. His hair is kept short, regulation-style, and is a mixture of black and grey, though the exact shade is often hidden beneath the helmet he rarely removes. His expression is almost always stern, his demeanor reserved, and his movements calculated, as though he is constantly evaluating threats and escape routes.

Minotaur is always seen in full Spec Ops gear, a suit of unknown origin that is as enigmatic as the man himself. The armor is jet-black, with no visible markings or insignia, save for a small, faded emblem on the chest plate that remains unidentifiable. The gear appears heavily customized, with various compartments and holsters for weapons and equipment. The armor's design is sleek but durable, built for both protection and agility, with reinforced plating around vital areas and a cloaking system integrated into the suit's framework. The helmet is the most distinctive piece, with a featureless visor that completely obscures his face, adding to his intimidating presence and further shrouding his identity.

# Personality:

Leonard "Minotaur" Galizia is a man of few words, often preferring silence over idle chatter. He communicates through short, concise statements, and his tone is always serious, reflecting a mind constantly at work. Despite his quiet nature, Minotaur exudes a sense of authority and command that demands respect. His comrades have come to rely on his unflinching resolve and his ability to remain calm under pressure, especially in the most intense combat situations.

Minotaur is driven by a deep-seated need to uncover the truth about his past. Suffering from severe amnesia, he remembers little of his life before a certain point—a fact that haunts him daily. What memories he retains are fragmented and often come to him in dreams or sudden flashes during combat. These memories are usually violent, filled with the sounds of battle, the faces of people he cannot name, and the overwhelming sense of betrayal. The only certainty Minotaur has is that he was once part of a highly specialized Spec Ops unit, and that something went catastrophically wrong. His quest for answers drives him forward, even as he struggles with the fear of what he might discover.

# Background:

The true origins of Minotaur remain a mystery, even to himself. His earliest memories are of waking up alone in the wilderness, his body battered and bruised, his mind clouded by confusion and pain. The only clues to his past were the scars on his body, the unfamiliar yet instinctual knowledge of combat, and the Spec Ops gear he wore—a gear that seemed to fit him perfectly, like a second skin.

Over time, Minotaur discovered that he possessed a vast array of combat skills, particularly in close-quarters combat and tactical operations. He moved like a soldier trained in the most rigorous conditions, able to handle advanced weaponry and execute complex maneuvers with ease. Yet, for all his prowess, there were gaps—crucial details about who he was, where he came from, and what had happened to him that remained frustratingly out of reach.

Minotaur’s journey has been one of survival and self-discovery. He has worked as a mercenary, taking on high-risk missions across the galaxy, often for those who require absolute discretion and effectiveness. His reputation has grown as a result, and his callsign, "Minotaur," is now known across many sectors as a force to be reckoned with.

Despite his outward confidence, Minotaur is plagued by doubts and fears. He questions whether his lost memories contain something too horrific to confront. Still, he pushes forward, using his missions as a way to unlock more pieces of his past. Each battle, each encounter brings him closer to the truth, though he knows that the answer might change him forever.

# Combat Abilities:

Minotaur is a formidable presence on the battlefield, particularly in environments that require stealth, precision, and the ability to operate independently. His Spec Ops training is evident in his every move—he is a master of close-quarters combat, capable of taking down multiple opponents in seconds. He favors tactical evasion and guerilla-style warfare, utilizing his environment to outmaneuver and outflank his enemies.

Minotaur's suit is equipped with advanced cloaking technology, allowing him to become nearly invisible when needed. This, combined with his knowledge of reconnaissance and infiltration, makes him a ghost on the battlefield—striking from the shadows and disappearing before the enemy can react.

While primarily a melee specialist, Minotaur is also proficient in a variety of ranged weapons, particularly those that can be used in close combat, such as shotguns, SMGs, and specialized pistols. His suit enhances his physical capabilities, providing increased strength, speed, and reflexes, making him a nearly unstoppable force in hand-to-hand combat.

# Current Status:

Minotaur currently operates as an independent Lancer, taking on contracts that align with his personal goals or offer the opportunity to uncover more about his past. He is highly sought after for missions that require his specific skill set, though he remains elusive and difficult to contact. Those who have worked with him know that he can be trusted to complete any mission, no matter how dangerous, as long as it brings him closer to the answers he seeks.

Despite his amnesia, Minotaur has a deep, almost instinctual sense of honor and duty. He refuses to abandon his comrades in the field and will go to great lengths to protect those he fights alongside. However, his ultimate loyalty is to his own quest for the truth—a quest that may one day force him to choose between the people he has come to care for and the answers he has sought for so long.

# Analysis:

Minotaur is a highly skilled and dangerous operative, driven by a past he cannot fully remember. His motivations are complex, and while he is an asset in any combat situation, his unpredictable nature and obsession with his lost memories make him a potential liability. Anyone seeking to hire or work with Minotaur should be aware of his personal quest and be prepared for the possibility that it could override any other mission objective.

# End of Dossier Entry.
