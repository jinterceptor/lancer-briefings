Jenson "Gargoyle" Hawkins was born on the Diasporan world of Thebos IV, a planet under Union control, though the real power lies with the local major corporations.

Jenson was orphaned at birth in one of the many mega-cities where 70% of the world's population resides. These cities are surrounded by lawless areas known as exclusion zones, where criminals, dissidents, and others are exiled to live under the harshest conditions.

To escape a life of poverty and the influence of underworld gangs, Jenson enlisted in the military at 16. After showing great promise and earning high marks in basic training, he was sent to the prestigious Thebos IV Military Academy, where he spent a couple of years learning how to operate a mech. Eventually, he was selected for the elite Covert Combat Units.

After passing the selection process, Jenson was assigned to the 350th CCU, where he met his trusted squadmates. He served with them for 10 years, completing many classified missions in the exclusion zones and even in mega-cities on the brink of rebellion or under terrorist attack.

This continued until Harrison Armory arrived with a fleet to fully bring the planet under Union control. While a few saw annexation by Harrison Armory as the best option, the majority of the major corporations banded together to resist, forming the Thebos Alliance to fight against the Loyalist Union Forces, who were "working" with the Harrison Armory legion. Thus began the Thebos IV Civil War.

The 350th CCU decided to join the Loyalist Union Forces, a decision that marked a turning point in Jenson's life. His home planet soon resembled one vast exclusion zone. The civil war was bloody and brutal, with infighting bringing the world to the brink of collapse, until finally, the Harrison Armory flag was planted across the entire planet.

Billions of lives were lost, including Jenson's squadmates in a failed mission. After this, Jenson took his mech and went AWOL, escaping on a Thebos Alliance ship bound for a colony world.
