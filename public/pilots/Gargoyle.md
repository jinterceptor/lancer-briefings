Jenson "Gargoyle" Hawkins born on the Diasporan world called Thebos IV that is on surface under Union control, albeit the power lies with the local Major Corporations.

Jenson was born an orphan in one of the many Mega Cities where the 70% of the worlds populace lives in, which is surrounded by lawless areas known as the exclusion zones, where criminals, dissidents, and others are exiled to live in harshest conditions.

To escape the poverty life and the underworld gangs, Jenson signed up for military service at 16. After showing great promise and high marks in basic training, sent to the prestigious Thebos IV military academy for a couple years where he learned how to use a mech, until chosen for selection for the elite Covert Combat Units.

After passing selection Jenson was assigned to the 350th CCU, where Jenson met his trusted squadmates, serving with them for 10 years, completing many classified missions in the exclusion zones to even Mega cities either on the brink of rebellion or under terrorist attacks.

It was until Harrison Armory came with a fleet to fully bring the planet under Union Control, while few decided best to be annexed by HA, majority of the Major Corporations banded together to fight back, creating the Thebos Alliance against the Loyalist Union Forces "working" with Harrison Armory legion, thus began the Thebos IV civil war.

The 350th CCU decided to join the Loyalist Union Forces, which began a turning point for Jensons life, as his home planet turned started to look like one whole exclusion zone, the civil war was bloody and brutal, the infighting itself brought the world to the brink, until finally the Harrison Armory flag was planted all across the world.

Billions of lives lost, including Jenson's Squadmates in a failed mission. After this, Jenson took his mech, and went AWOL leaving on a Thebos Alliance ship leaving for a colony world.
