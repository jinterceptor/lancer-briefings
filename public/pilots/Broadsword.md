Broadsword distinguished himself as a highly effective freelance asset, renowned for his sharp intelligence and unmatched precision. Specializing in both intelligence gathering and targeted eliminations, his skill set quickly set him apart in the world of covert operations.

As a reward for his exceptional performance on a particularly challenging mission, Broadsword was offered a significant career advancement: the opportunity to become a freelance Lancer pilot. This new role allowed him to pilot advanced frames and apply his tactical expertise in a broader range of scenarios.
